# Course Index

Quick reference for all courses in this collection.

## Beginner Courses

### [LLM Course (Transformers)](./llm-course.pdf) | 7.7 MB
- **Focus**: Natural Language Processing fundamentals
- **Topics**: Transformers architecture, attention mechanisms, tokenization, BERT, GPT
- **Duration**: ~40 hours estimated
- **Source**: [huggingface/course](https://github.com/huggingface/course)

### [Smol Course](./smol-course.pdf) | 1.2 MB
- **Focus**: Small Language Models & efficiency
- **Topics**: Model compression, quantization, edge deployment, efficient inference
- **Duration**: ~15 hours estimated
- **Source**: [huggingface/smol-course](https://github.com/huggingface/smol-course)

## Intermediate Courses

### [Agents Course](./agents-course.pdf) | 6.5 MB
- **Focus**: Building AI Agents
- **Topics**: LLM agents, tool use, planning, multi-agent systems, LangChain
- **Duration**: ~30 hours estimated
- **Source**: [huggingface/agents-course](https://github.com/huggingface/agents-course)

### [Audio Course](./audio-course.pdf) | 2.4 MB
- **Focus**: Audio Transformers
- **Topics**: Speech recognition, audio classification, Whisper, music generation
- **Duration**: ~20 hours estimated
- **Source**: [huggingface/audio-transformers-course](https://github.com/huggingface/audio-transformers-course)

### [Computer Vision Course](./computer-vision-course.pdf) | 14.8 MB
- **Focus**: Vision Transformers
- **Topics**: Image classification, object detection, ViT, segmentation, DINO
- **Duration**: ~45 hours estimated
- **Source**: [huggingface/computer-vision-course](https://github.com/huggingface/computer-vision-course)
